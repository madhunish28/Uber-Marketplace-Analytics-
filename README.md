# Executive Marketplace Analytics Dashboard (Uber Case Study)

An end-to-end Business Intelligence project that analyzes **103,024+ ride-booking records** to uncover operational bottlenecks, revenue leakage, customer behavior, and marketplace performance using **SQL, Excel, and Tableau**.

This project simulates an executive analytics solution for a ride-sharing platform by transforming raw transactional data into interactive dashboards and actionable business insights.



## 📌 Project Objectives

* Analyze ride-booking performance across the marketplace.
* Identify revenue leakage caused by unsuccessful bookings.
* Evaluate vehicle portfolio performance.
* Monitor customer and driver satisfaction.
* Understand operational friction and cancellation patterns.
* Build executive-level dashboards for business decision-making.



## 🛠 Tech Stack

* **SQL (MySQL)** – Data modeling, analytical queries, and business views
* **Microsoft Excel** – Data preparation and preprocessing
* **Tableau** – Interactive dashboards and business intelligence
* **GitHub** – Project documentation and version control


# 📊 Dashboard Architecture

The solution is organized into **five executive dashboards**, each focusing on a different business function.

| Dashboard                               | Business Focus                                                                  |
| --------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Executive Marketplace Overview**      | Ride volume trends, booking fulfillment, and executive KPIs                     |
| **Vehicle Portfolio Analytics**         | Fleet performance, ride distance, and service quality across vehicle categories |
| **Revenue & Monetization Dynamics**     | Revenue trends, payment behavior, and high-value customers                      |
| **Marketplace Friction & Leakage**      | Customer cancellations, driver cancellations, and revenue leakage analysis      |
| **Quality Assurance & Trust Analytics** | Driver ratings, customer ratings, and marketplace satisfaction metrics          |


# 📈 Executive KPIs

This dashboard includes executive-level business metrics such as:

* Total Bookings
* Total Booking Value
* Successful Booking Value
* Revenue Leakage
* Revenue Leakage Rate (%)
* Marketplace Fulfillment Rate
* Average Customer Rating
* Average Driver Rating


# 🔍 Business Questions Answered

The project answers the following key business questions:

1. How does ride demand change over time?
2. What percentage of bookings are successfully fulfilled?
3. Which vehicle categories generate the highest ride distance?
4. Which vehicle categories deliver the best customer experience?
5. Which payment methods generate the highest revenue?
6. Who are the highest-value customers?
7. What are the leading causes of customer and driver cancellations?
8. How much revenue is lost because of unsuccessful bookings?
9. How do customer and driver ratings compare across the marketplace?
10. Which operational bottlenecks most affect marketplace efficiency?
